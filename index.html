MAINBRANDER

Dior

Full Name: Christian Dior SE

Founder: Christian Dior

Established Date: December 16, 1946

Parent Company: LVMH

Chairman: Bernard Arnault, who also heads LVMH, the world's largest luxury group. Dior itself holds 42.36% shares of and 59.01% voting rights within LVMH. Arnault held an additional 5.28% of shares and 4.65% of votes directly.

Creative Director: Maria Grazia Chiuri

Best Known for: Extravagance in women’s wear in a post-war time when women had been used to shorter and boxier skirts, which had their roots in the war-time rationing of fabric.

About: Christian Dior, more commonly known as Dior, is a luxury goods company famed for revolutionizing the women’s fashion industry. The brand has been cited as the catalyst for Paris’ revival as the fashion capital of the world after it fell from its prominent position after WWII.

Chanel

Full Name: Chanel

Founder: Coco Chanel

Established Date: 1910

Parent Company: Chanel Limited. Chanel has been privately owned by the Wertheimer family for almost a century.

Chairman: Alain Wertheimer

Creative Director: Virginie Viard

Best Known for: Timeless designs, trademark suits and little black dresses.

About: Chanel transformed the women’s fashion industry by bringing elegance and simple design to over-designed, opulent fashion. The label has continued to break down barriers in fashion while still maintaining the elegance on which it was built.

YSL (Saint Laurent)

Full Name: Yves Saint Laurent

Founders: Yves Saint Laurent & Pierre Bergé

Established Date: 1961

Parent Company: Kering

CEO: Francesca Bellettini

Creative Director: Anthony Vaccarello

Best Known for: Brought art to fashion. Popularization of women's trousers for all occasions. Brought diversity on the runaway.

About: One word to describe Saint Laurent would be “bold”. In many ways, Saint Laurent was ahead of his times. In an era when it was unthinkable for women to wear men’s suits, he introduced the Le Smoking suit. In keeping with the new wave of feminism in the 60s, Yves Saint Laurent introduced tops constructed using see-through fabric that revealed women’s breasts. It was a risque move, but this collection spoke highly of how the designer put the woman first in his designs.

Celine

Full Name: Celine (formerly spelled Céline, and stylized in all caps)

Founder: Céline Vipiana

Established Date: 1945

Parent Company: LVMH

CEO: Séverine Merle

Creative Director: Hedi Slimane

Best Known for: Celine is now known for its simplicity that involves heritage, elegance and a hint of androgyny. Thanks to its creative director Phoebe Philo, the once failing brand is now an icon for minimalism and grown up dressing.

About: Originally started as children’s shoe business, the French company expanded during the sixties into adult footwear, accessories and a women’s ready-to-wear sports collection. With the expansion, Celine opened distribution in Japan, making them the first luxury brands to do so.
